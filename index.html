Science, Technology and Education

The sciences of nutrition and movement are fascinating.


We believe that knowing the why's behind well known advice is key to motivation and participation and that the application of knowledge needs to be sustainable, practical, sociable and fun. We achieve this through classes, presentations, workshops and the use of technology such as apps for phones, 3d animations and games.


We are a UK registered not for profit community interest company and really need volunteers in the following projects

  • Science
      • Nutritionist with minumum of BSc Hons Nutrition to help edit and review content
  • Technology
      • Xcode/Swift developers to help with the creation of our meal planning app
      Unity/c# developers to help with the creation of our animations, interactive books and games
      3D artists familiar with blender
  • Events Management
      • Joint heatlh, gentle movement and lunch for the over 60s
      Bone mass awareness for teens
      The importance of diet and excercise for people with depression

If you would love to see these beneficial projects out in the community and have a skill or two that can help we would love to hear from you
